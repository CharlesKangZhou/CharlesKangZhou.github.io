Biography

I am currently a third-year (2019-2020) Ph.D. candidate at ShanghaiTech University, advised by Prof. Shenghua Gao. I am also a member of the iMED Group, working with Prof. Jiang Liu, Prof. Jianlong Yang, and Prof. Jun Cheng. Before that, I obtained my bachelor's degree from Northwestern Polytechnical University in 2017.


I am interested in medical image analysis (especially the retinal image), and unsupervised anomaly detection. My current research focuses on unsupervised anomaly detection for retinal images.
