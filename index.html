Biography

I am currently a fourth-year (2020-2021) Ph.D. candidate at ShanghaiTech University, advised by Prof. Shenghua Gao. I am also a member of the iMED Group, working with Prof. Jiang Liu. Before that, I obtained my bachelor's degree from Northwestern Polytechnical University in 2017.


I am interested in the understanding of medical images, especially the retinal fundus image and OCT images. Recently, my research focuses on anomaly detection in medical images.
