Biography

I am currently a final-year (2021-2022) Ph.D. candidate at ShanghaiTech University. Before that, I received my bachelor degree from Northwestern Polytechnical University (NWPU) in 2017.


My research direction is intelligent medical image analysis, with a primary focus on ophthalmic images. Recently, I focus on developing image anomaly detection methods and their application in the medical image. My research interests are as follows:

- Technique: Open-Set Learning, Anomaly Detection, Domain Adaptation, Semi-supervised Learning

- Organ: Eye, Brain

- Disease: Diabetic Retinopathy (DR), Age-related Macular Degeneration (AMD), Glaucoma

- Imaging Modality: Color Fundus, OCT, MRI

- Medical Imaging: OCT Imaging, Photoacoustic Imaging
