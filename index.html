About

I am currently a resarch intern at KooLab in Kujiale.com, working with Dr. Rui Tang and Prof. Zihan Zhou. Before that, I obtained my master's degree from ShanghaiTech University working with Prof. Shenghua Gao, and obtained my bachelor's degree from Northwestern Polytechnical University.


I am interested in computer vision and machine learning, particularly in the areas of 3D Vision. My current research focuses on recovering 3D geometry from images by utilizing shape regularities.
